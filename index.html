Welcome to Clean City!

How to play

To play the game move your player using the arrow keys


Your goal is to collect as many garbage items in the time you have and take them to the trash before the time is up


If you score the minimum the required amount of garbage items collected, you'll win


Remember! If you don't put tthem in the trash can, they don't count!
